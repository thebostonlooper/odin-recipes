Avocado Toast

Return to Main Page

Description

From the streets of yuppie-ville comes this new age breakfast enjoyed by 20-somethings that still aren't contributing enough to their retirement account to afford $2 avocados is one of my personal favorite ways to start the day: Avocado Toast.

Ingredients

  • Slice of Wheat Bread
  • Avocado
  • Egg
  • Red Pepper Flakes

Steps

  1. Start cooking the egg on the stovetop
  2. Toast the slice of Bread
  3. Mash up the avocado in the bowl
  4. Once all three are done, start to spread the mashed Avocado on the toast
  5. Add the egg on top of the avocado
  6. Add the red pepper flakes
  7. Enjoy!!!
