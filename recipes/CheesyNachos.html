Cheesy Nachos

Return to Main Page

Description

From late night snack to post-bar necessity, a true classic that can only be enjoyed until the mid-30s heartburn starts to rear its ugly head - the Cheesy Nachos.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla Chips
  • Shredded Mixed Cheese
  • (Optional) Sliced Jalepenos

Steps

  1. Layer enough chips on your metal sheet to cover the bottom of the plate
  2. Add cheese to all of the chips so no gaps remain (as a wise woman once told me don't be stinge on the cheese)
  3. Repeat steps 1 + 2 until the desired amount of Nachos are ready
  4. Add metal sheet to toaster oven and put on toast setting until all layers of cheese are sufficiently melted
  5. Eat Up!!!
